⚠ Critical Context — LTC Coding Note

GPAD does not have a direct LTC flag. Conservative (red) = explicitly LTC-coded categories: Structured Medication Review, Care Home Needs Assessment, Care Home Visit, Patient contact during Care Home Round. Mid tier adds General Consultation Routine & Planned Clinics. Upper = all GP 30+ min. Low conservative figures reflect GPAD coding practice, not clinical activity — this is a key data quality baseline finding for ENN.
